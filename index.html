Key Items are placed in their zones before the rest of the seed is generated
If key items are not in their zones, duplicate copies will be placed.
Zone 1: Morph, Charge, Explosives, Missile
Zone 2: Speed, Varia, Boost Ball and 3 Energy Tanks
Zone 3: Gravity, Space Jump
The gray doors preventing backtracking from zone 2 to zone 1 will now be red doors.
The game will be unaletered. If you leave an item in a past zone, you softlock.

Generation can take up to 90 seconds, and your browser might stop responding.
